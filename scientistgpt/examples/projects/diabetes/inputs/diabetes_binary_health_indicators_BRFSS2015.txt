This csv file contains health survey responses downloaded from the Kaggle's Diabetes Health Indicators Dataset.

The csv file is a clean dataset of 253,680 responses (rows) and 22 features (columns).
All rows with missing values were removed from the original dataset; the current file contains no missing values.

The columns in the dataset are:

#1 `Diabetes_binary`: (int, categorical) "Diabetes" (0=no, 1=yes)
#2 `HighBP`: (int, categorical) "High Blood Pressure" (0=no, 1=yes)
#3 `HighChol`: (int, categorical) "High Cholesterol" (0=no, 1=yes)
#4 `CholCheck`: (int, categorical) "Cholesterol Check" - did you checked for Cholesterol in 5 years (0=no, 1=yes)
#5 `BMI`: (int, numerical) "Body Mass Index"
#6 `Smoker`: (int, categorical) "Smoker" - are you smoking (0=no, 1=yes)
#7 `Stroke`: (int, categorical) "Stroke" - did you had stroke in the past (0=no, 1=yes)
#8 `HeartDiseaseorAttack': (int, categorical) "Coronary Heart Disease (CHD) or Myocardial Infarction (MI)" - did you had one of the mentioned heart problems in the past (0=no, 1=yes)
#9 `PhysActivity`: (int, categorical) "Physical Activity" - did you engaged in a physical activity in the past 30 days (0=no, 1=yes)
#10 `Fruits`: (int, categorical) "Fruit Consumption" - do you consume one fruit or more each day (0=no, 1=yes)
#11 `Veggies`: (int, categorical) "Vegetable Consumption" - do you consume one vegetable or more each day (0=no, 1=yes)
#12 `HvyAlcoholConsump` (int, categorical) "Heavy Drinker" - are you a heavy alcohol drinker (0=no, 1=yes)
#13 `AnyHealthcare` (int, categorical) "Healthcare Coverage" - do you have any kind of health care coverage (0=no, 1=yes)
#14 `NoDocbcCost` (int, categorical) "No Financial Access to Medical Care" - Was there a time in the past 12 months when you needed to see a doctor but could not because of cost (0=no, 1=yes)
#15 `GenHlth` (int, ordinal) "General Health" - self-reported health (1=excellent, 2=very good, 3=good, 4=fair, 5=poor)
#16 `MentHlth` (int, ordinal) "Mental Health" - how many days during the past 30 days was your mental health not good? (1-30 days)
#17 `PhysHlth` (int, ordinal) "Physical Health" - How many days during the past 30 days was your physical health not good? (1-30 days)
#18 `DiffWalk` (int, categorical) "Difficulty Walking" - Do you have serious difficulty walking or climbing stairs? (0=no, 1=yes)
#19 `Sex` (int, categorical) "Sex" (0=female, 1=male)
#20 `Age` (int, ordinal) "Age" - 13-level age category in intervals of 5 years (1=18-24, 2=25-29, ..., 12=75-79, 13=80 or older)
#21 `Education` (int, ordinal) "Education" - level on a scale of 1-6
#22 `Income` (int, ordinal) "Income" - level on a scale of 1-8
